Admin work is the tax on being a business owner. Tax prep, contract review, expense categorization, insurance renewals, compliance paperwork — nobody starts a company to do this stuff, but it consumes entire weekends if you let it pile up.

Claude can handle the grunt work. Not the judgment calls — those are still yours — but the reading, organizing, categorizing, and drafting that makes admin so time-consuming.

## Contract Review

You get a 15-page vendor agreement. Your lawyer charges $500/hour. Before you send it to legal, let Claude do the first pass.

<PromptBlock
  title="Contract review first pass"
  prompt="Review this contract and give me a plain-English assessment:\n\n[upload or paste the contract]\n\n1. **What am I agreeing to?** (summary in 3-5 bullets — no legalese)\n2. **Term and termination** — How long is this for? ## Common Mistakes

**Don't rely on Claude for legal or tax advice.** Claude can read, organize, summarize, and flag — but it cannot replace professional advice for decisions with legal or financial consequences. Use Claude to prepare, not to decide.

**Don't upload confidential documents without considering your data policies.** If you're reviewing a contract with sensitive terms, make sure you're comfortable with how Claude handles that data. Business-tier plans have stronger data retention policies.

**Don't skip the human review.** Claude might miss nuance in a contract that a lawyer would catch. Claude might miscategorize an expense that your accountant would flag. The value is in the speed, not in eliminating professional oversight.
