# /repurpose

Take a YouTube script and repurpose it across your content platforms. Total editing time: ~20 minutes.

## Input

**Source file:** $ARGUMENTS

If no file provided, ask which script from `output/` to repurpose.

## Instructions

1. Read the source YouTube script
2. Generate all outputs below in sequence
3. Save each to the appropriate output file

---

## Output 1: Newsletter Post

**File:** `output/newsletter-[topic-slug].md`

### Structure
```
# [Title - Curiosity-Driving, Keyword-Aware]

[HOOK - 2-3 short paragraphs]
Open with the core insight or a brief story.
Ultra-short paragraphs. One sentence per paragraph is fine.
This appears in email preview — make it count.

---

[MAIN CONTENT]
Reframe the video content for reading:
- More reflective tone than video
- Go deeper on one aspect rather than covering everything
- Break into scannable sections with subheadings
- Include specific examples and numbers

---

## The Takeaway

[What they can do with this TODAY]
Be specific. One clear action.

---

I made a video going deeper on this: [Video title + link]

---

[SOFT CTA - Choose based on content type]
```

### Guidelines
- 600-1,000 words (shorter than the video script)
- Don't cover everything — pick the meatiest 60%
- More personal/reflective than the video
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- End with video link + relevant CTA
- Title should work for newsletter discovery/search

---

## Output 2: Social Posts (5 Posts)

**File:** `output/social-posts-[topic-slug].md`

Generate 5 distinct posts from the video content, posted throughout the week.

### 5 Post Types

```
## Post 1: Key Insight
[The single most shareable insight from the video]

## Post 2: Contrarian Take
"Most people think [X]. But [Y]."

## Post 3: Tactical Tip
"Try this: [specific action]"

## Post 4: Personal Reflection
"I used to [X]. Then I learned [Y]."

## Post 5: Question / Engagement
"Question: [specific question related to content]"
```

### Guidelines
- Each post stands completely alone (no context needed)
- Under 280 characters for maximum engagement
- No hashtags
- Link to video/post on 2-3 of them (not all)
- More casual than the newsletter
- Personality shows through

---

## Output 3: LinkedIn Post

**File:** `output/linkedin-[topic-slug].md`

### Structure
```
[HOOK LINE - stop the scroll, 1 sentence]

[2-3 short paragraphs expanding the insight]
- Lead with the insight, not context
- Include one specific number or example
- Professional but not corporate
- Line breaks between every 1-2 sentences

[TAKEAWAY or QUESTION]

---
Full breakdown: [video link]
```

### Guidelines
- 150-300 words max
- No hashtag spam (0-2 max)
- Heavy line breaks (algorithm favors this)
- Always end with video link

---

## Output 4: Short-Form Video Script

**File:** `output/short-[topic-slug].md`

Extract ONE compelling point for a vertical short.

### Structure
```
[HOOK - first 2 seconds]
Pattern interrupt or bold statement

[CORE INSIGHT - 30-45 seconds]
ONE point from the main video
Specific, actionable, or surprising

[CTA - final 5 seconds]
"Full breakdown on my channel"
```

### Guidelines
- Under 60 seconds total
- Front-load the value
- Fast pace, no filler
- Pick the most visual/demonstrable point
